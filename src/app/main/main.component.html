Welcome to
Open Source Music!

This service is intended for musicians and sound engineers who want to share their projects with other people, as well as for beginning musicians, who want to get experience by exploring other people's projects.

Open Source Music
allows you

  • publish your music projects, so everyone can see them
  • explore other people projects
  • invite users to your project
  • fill your project with files. You can attach music files, project files, album artwork, whatever you like
to get started.
